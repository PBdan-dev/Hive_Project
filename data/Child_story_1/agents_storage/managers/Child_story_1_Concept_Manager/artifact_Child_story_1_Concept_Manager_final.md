### 💡 UNIFIED THEMATIC FRAMEWORK
- **Core Moral:** *True courage isn’t the absence of fear—it’s choosing to do what’s right even when you’re afraid.*
- **Integration Method:** Show-don’t-tell. The moral emerges through Barnaby’s behavioral shifts: freezing vs. fleeing, anchoring himself, bracing, and leaping despite trembling. No direct moralizing dialogue.
- **Audience Resonance:** Validates childhood anxiety, reframes fear as a natural companion to bravery, and demonstrates that impact scales with intention, not size.
- **Narrative Anchor:** The lost blue button serves as the tangible catalyst. Retrieving it symbolizes Barnaby reclaiming agency over his own limitations.
