### ⏱️ PACING STRATEGY NOTES FOR DRAFTING
- **Sentence Architecture:** Begin with longer, flowing sentences → compress to staccato phrasing during obstacles → expand again at climax/resolution for emotional breathing room.
- **Tension Curve:** Linear rise with three micro-peaks (carpet, wind, cat) culminating in the beam/button climax. Prevents mid-story sag.
- **Moral Integration:** Never preach. The lesson emerges through physical action (freezing vs. fleeing, anchoring, leaping despite fear) and is mirrored in the closing image.
- **Word Control:** Each beat includes strict allocation. Dialogue will be minimal (max 3 lines total) to preserve descriptive/adventure space within the 1000-word cap.
