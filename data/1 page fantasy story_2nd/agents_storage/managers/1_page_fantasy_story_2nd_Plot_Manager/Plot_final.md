## 3. HYBRIDIZED BEAT SHEET (8 CHAPTERS)
*Structure merges Three-Act pacing with granular chapter milestones. Names and mechanics standardized.*

### **ACT I: SETUP & THRESHOLD (~Ch 1–2)**

#### **CHAPITRE 1 : Le Fil Cassé**
*   **Milestone:** Introduction of Sylve scrubbing residual echoes in the *Cimetière des Oubliés*. Establishes magic via breath: she inhales sorrow to stabilize a decaying wall. Hints of her dark past (the "Silence Plague") surface as fragmented visions.
*   **Inciting Incident:** A sealed artifact—the **Fragment Primaire**—arrives at her workshop. Upon contact, it resonates violently, projecting a vision of a looming **Dissonance Wave** threatening to unravel Aethel.
*   **Turning Point:** **Gardiens du Fil** corner her using a resonance snare. In panic, Sylve inhales deeply, absorbing their attack but triggering a localized gravity glitch. She escapes but realizes the Fragment is dragging her toward the *Cité des Ombles*. She crosses the threshold, fleeing the border settlement.

#### **CHAPITRE 2 : Les Ruines de l'Oubli**
*   **Milestone:** Journey across landscapes shaped by collapsed timelines. Sylve meets **Kael**, a rogue archivist trading in orphaned echoes. Worldbuilding expands: society hoards echoes as currency and mortar.
*   **Complication:** They seek refuge in a safehouse revealed to be a hunter's trap. Kael initially intends to hand Sylve over for reward.
*   **Turning Point:** Hunters breach the hideout. Sylve stabilizes a collapsing echo-zone instead of destroying it, saving Kael. He witnesses her restorative potential and switches sides, forging a fragile alliance. **Elara** (Mentor) intercepts them, revealing the Plague was a ritual failure covered up by the Council.

### **ACT II: CONFRONTATION & MIDPOINT (~Ch 3–6)**

#### **CHAPITRE 3 : La Voix du Verre**
*   **Milestone:** Training sequence. Elara teaches controlled resonance. Flashbacks reveal the truth: Sylve consented to be the primary vessel for the Order to harvest children's memories, believing it would save the city. She wasn't just a victim; she bears complicity.
*   **Power Mechanics:** Sylve practices *Aspiration*. She learns that suppression amplifies damage; only intentional resonance heals.
*   **Turning Point:** Ambushed by harvesters. Sylve fully activates her power, erasing a foundational echo to break the siege. The resulting fracture injures Kael. She realizes her ability rewrites causal threads but exacts a toll: each rupture costs her a personal memory. A new fissure appears on her temple.

#### **CHAPITRE 4 : L'Encre Noire** *(Midpoint)*
*   **Milestone:** Infiltration of the subterranean archive where echoes are bound into glass-texts. Sylve uncovers records proving the "preservation" ritual was actually a cage for **Le Silence**, a natural corrective wave that resets overloaded realities.
*   **Revelation:** Touching a text containing her erased childhood memory, Sylve experiences a full vision. She chose the vessel role to prevent total annihilation. Her guilt transforms into purpose: she must not stop the wave, but guide it.
*   **Turning Point:** Hunters breach the archive. High-speed chase ensues. Goal shifts from survival to active restoration.

#### **CHAPITRE 5 : Le Pont des Soupirs**
*   **Milestone:** Traversal of a bridge maintained by collective sorrow-echoes. Sylve must modulate her breath carefully to avoid collapse while evading pursuit. Kael provides tactical support and emotional grounding.
*   **All Is Lost:** A hunter detonates a resonance charge mid-crossing. To save Kael and Elara, Sylve absorbs the blast entirely. She survives but permanently loses a core memory (her mother's face/name).
*   **Dark Night:** Elara falls protecting Sylve from a follow-up strike. Sylve panics, siphons too deeply, and accidentally erases recent memories from Kael. Believing she is inherently destructive, she retreats alone into a dead-zone. Confronts her trauma: realizes suppression caused the decay; integration is the only path. Reclaims agency.

#### **CHAPITRE 6 : Le Cœur du Résonateur**
*   **Milestone:** Sylve returns to the epicenter, the **Fracture Spire**. Discovery: *Le Silence* isn't a monster but a healing tide. The Council's attempt to freeze time caused stagnation and decay.
*   **Confrontation:** Faces the **Grand Tisseur**, now a hollow echo-wraith. He offers Sylve restoration of her lost memories and dominion over a stabilized, static world.
*   **Turning Point:** Sylve refuses. Recognizing forced permanence as spiritual death, she prepares to trigger a controlled unspooling. She accepts her scars as integral, not defective.

### **ACT III: CLIMAX & RESOLUTION (~Ch 7–8)**

#### **CHAPITRE 7 : La Rupture** *(Climax)*
*   **Milestone:** Sylve channels her power to shatter the central anchor binding the false timeline. Reality fractures and reforms: echoes materialize visibly, gravity shifts, memories bleed into the environment. Kael holds off remaining enforcers.
*   **Action:** Tempted to erase all pain, Sylve instead weaves her trauma into the new world's foundation. She opens herself completely, siphoning the dissonant frequencies and restructuring them into harmony.
*   **Resolution of Conflict:** The wave stabilizes; reality knits back together fluidly. The wraith dissolves/freed. Sylve risks cognitive dissolution but succeeds through acceptance.

#### **CHAPITRE 8 : L'Aube des Échos Libres**
*   **Milestone:** Aftermath. Survivors regain memories peacefully. The Council's grip collapses as truth surfaces. Companions reconcile; lost memories return organically through stabilized resonance.
*   **Character Closure:** Sylve stops hiding. Establishes a sanctuary for Echo-Touched youth, teaching controlled resonance as healing.
*   **Final Image:** Mirrors opening. Sylve stands in a quiet room, not scrubbing away echoes, but listening to them. She hears an unfamiliar echo in the wind—a reminder that stories continue. Steps forward as a conscious weaver. Tone: Grounded, hopeful, emotionally precise.

## 4. STYLISTIC & EXECUTION DIRECTIVES

### **Prose Guidelines**
*   **Acoustic Metaphors:** Describe emotions and actions through sound. Pain is a "discordant screech"; peace is a "humming chord"; lies are "muffled whispers."
*   **Sensory Weight:** Emphasize the physical sensation of magic. Inhaling echoes feels like cold water filling lungs; exhaling feels like warmth radiating from bones. Fissures burn or tingle.
*   **Restraint:** Avoid exposition dumps. Lore is revealed through action (e.g., Kael mapping zones, Sylve touching glass-texts). Show the cost of magic immediately after use.
*   **French Cadence:** Maintain poetic rhythm suitable for French YA. Use concise sentences for tension; flowing, rhythmic syntax for magical moments.

### **Pacing Strategy**
*   **Lean Structure:** Every scene must advance plot, deepen lore, or push character growth. No filler.
*   **Rising Stakes:** Start with local survival (Ch 1), expand to regional threat (Ch 4), culminate in existential reality stabilization (Ch 7).
*   **Emotional Beats:** Balance external action with internal processing. Sylve's memory losses must hurt narratively, not just mechanically.

### **Risk Mitigation**
*   **Name Consistency:** Strict adherence to **Sylve**, **Kael**, **Elara**, **Aethel**.
*   **Magic Limits:** Sylve cannot solve every problem with magic. Physical limitations and memory costs force creative solutions and reliance on allies.
*   **Scope Control:** No empire-building. Focus remains tightly on Sylve's identity and the immediate stabilization of the Dissonance Wave.
