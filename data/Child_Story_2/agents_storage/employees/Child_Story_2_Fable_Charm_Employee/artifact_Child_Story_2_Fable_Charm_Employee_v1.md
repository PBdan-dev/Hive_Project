### ⚙️ MAGICAL MECHANICS
**Activation Trigger:**  
- Awakens when addressed with genuine curiosity or kindness (e.g., “Hello, little friend,” spoken softly)  
- Remains dormant if handled roughly, ignored, or treated as a mere toy  

**Core Abilities:**  
1. **Thread Weaving:** Projects luminous crimson threads from its joints to bridge gaps, lift light objects, or untangle knots  
2. **Echo Mimicry:** Replicates natural sounds (wind, bird calls, water flow) to communicate with forest creatures or mask footsteps  
3. **Shadow Casting:** Shapes brief, harmless light/shadow illusions to reveal hidden paths or calm startled animals  

**Limitations & Rules:**  
- **Energy Drain:** Overusing abilities causes stiffness and silence; requires rest to recover  
- **Recharge Condition:** Regains vitality through quiet companionship, shared stories, or patient stillness  
- **Communication Progression:** Begins non-verbal; gains simple words/phrases only as trust and mutual respect deepen  
- **No Force Principle:** Magic cannot compel, harm, or override free will; only assists, guides, or reveals
